Sensitive Skin

Goal: Soothe, protect, and strengthen the skin barrier.

Recommended Ingredients: Aloe vera, Centella asiatica (Cica), ceramides, hyaluronic acid, low-dose niacinamide, oat extract.

Avoid: Fragrance, alcohol, harsh exfoliants, strong actives, overwashing.

Routine:
1. Gentle, fragrance-free cleanser
2. Soothing toner or essence
3. Calming serum (ceramides or low-dose niacinamide)
4. Barrier-protecting moisturizer
5. Mineral sunscreen if going outside
