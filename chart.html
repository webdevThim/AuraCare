Identify Your Skin Type

Key Question 1: After washing your face and leaving it bare for 30–60 minutes, how does your skin feel?

Feels greasy → Oily Skin
Feels tight or flaky → Dry Skin
Feels irritated or red → Sensitive Skin

Key Question 2: How does your skin react to new products?

Breaks out or burns easily → Sensitive Skin
Gets rough or patchy → Dry Skin
Gets shiny quickly → Oily Skin

Key Question 3: What do your pores look like?

Large, visible pores → Oily Skin
Small, almost invisible pores → Dry Skin
Normal pores but reacts easily → Sensitive Skin
