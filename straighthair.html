Straight Hair

Goal: Keep straight hair smooth, shiny, and healthy.

Recommended Products: Lightweight shampoos, smoothing conditioners, hair serums.

Avoid: Heavy styling products that weigh hair down, excessive heat.

Routine:
1. Wash hair 2-3 times a week with a gentle shampoo.
2. Condition regularly to prevent dryness and frizz.
3. Apply a lightweight hair serum or leave-in conditioner for shine.
4. Minimize heat styling; use a heat protectant if needed.
5. Trim regularly to avoid split ends.

Hair Care Tips:
- Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid breakage.
- Avoid overwashing to maintain natural oils.
- Apply lightweight serum to ends to prevent dryness.
- Limit heat styling and always use heat protectant.
