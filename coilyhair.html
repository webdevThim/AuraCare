Coily Hair

Goal: Maintain tight coils, moisture, and minimize breakage.

Recommended Products: Sulfate-free shampoo, moisturizing conditioner, curl cream, oils for sealing moisture.

Avoid: Harsh shampoos, over-manipulation, heat styling.

Routine:
1. Wash hair 1-2 times per week with gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
2. Deep condition frequently to retain moisture.
3. Apply leave-in conditioner and curl cream to damp hair.
4. Use protective styles and minimize combing when dry.
5. Seal with natural oils to lock in moisture.

Hair Care Tips:
- Sleep with a satin bonnet or pillowcase to reduce friction.
- Avoid tight hairstyles that stress the roots.
- Detangle gently with fingers or a wide-tooth comb.
- Keep hair hydrated by refreshing with water and leave-in daily.
- Protective styles help prevent breakage.
