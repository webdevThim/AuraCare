Curly Hair

Goal: Maintain curl definition, prevent frizz, and retain moisture.

Recommended Products: Sulfate-free shampoo, hydrating conditioner, curl cream, leave-in conditioner.

Avoid: Harsh shampoos, excessive heat, brushing dry curls.

Routine:
1. Wash hair 1-2 times per week with sulfate-free shampoo.
2. Deep condition regularly to maintain hydration.
3. Apply curl cream or leave-in conditioner on damp hair.
4. Finger-comb or use a wide-tooth comb to detangle.
5. Air dry or diffuse to enhance curl pattern.

Hair Care Tips:
- Sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz.
- Avoid brushing dry curls.
- Refresh curls with water or leave-in conditioner.
- Finger-comb or use a wide-tooth comb to detangle.
- Deep condition weekly.
