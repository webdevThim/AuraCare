Wavy Hair

Goal: Enhance natural waves and reduce frizz.

Recommended Products: Moisturizing shampoo, curl-enhancing conditioner, lightweight mousse or curl cream.

Avoid: Heavy oils that weigh hair down, harsh heat styling.

Routine:
1. Wash hair 2-3 times per week with gentle shampoo.
2. Condition to maintain moisture and definition.
3. Apply curl cream or mousse while hair is damp.
4. Air dry or diffuse to keep natural waves intact.
5. Trim regularly to prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips:
- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair.
- Avoid brushing dry waves to prevent frizz.
- Apply lightweight curl cream for definition.
- Sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce friction.
- Refresh waves with a mist of water or leave-in conditioner.
