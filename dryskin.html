Dry Skin

Goal: Soothe and hydrate the skin.

Recommended Ingredients: Aloe vera, glycerin, ceramides, collagen.

Avoid: Fragrance, alcohol, harsh exfoliants, strong actives, overwashing.

Routine:
To treat dry facial skin, use products with moisturizing, hydrating, and barrier-repairing ingredients such as glycerin, hyaluronic acid, ceramides, and squalane. You may also incorporate shea butter, niacinamide, and natural oils like rosehip or coconut oil. Avoid harsh ingredients such as alcohol, fragrance, and salicylic acid.

1. Use a gentle cleanser.
2. Apply a hydrating toner or gel (aloe-based products are great).
3. Follow with a moisturizing serum.
4. Finish with a light moisturizer.
