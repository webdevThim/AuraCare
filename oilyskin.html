Oily Skin

Goal: Treat oily skin, remove excess sebum and avoid buildups.

Recommended Ingredients: Salicylic acid, niacinamide, UV blockers.

Avoid: Oily foods, alcohol-based toners, heavy oil-based products.

Routine:
1. Double-cleanse using an oil cleanser first, followed by a foam cleanser (preferably one with salicylic acid to help break down excess oil and sebum).

2. Use a toner with niacinamide to brighten the skin and help balance your skin’s pH levels.

3. Use a lightweight moisturizer if your skin feels tight after your routine. If not, you may skip this step.

4. Apply sunscreen if you’re going outdoors. If you’re staying indoors, you can skip this step, as sunscreen may sometimes clog pores.

Important: Do not use products containing Vitamin C together with salicylic acid or niacinamide, as this combination may irritate or damage your skin. Consult your dermatologist for accurate recommendations.
